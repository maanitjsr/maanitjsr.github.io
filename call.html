Instructions for Authors (for Submission of Full Length Articles)

Please note the following before submitting the article.
  • ORIGINAL AND HIGH QUALITY RESEARCH PAPERS are invited to submit in MAA 2025.
  • Submissions must not substantially duplicate work that any of the authors has published elsewhere or has submitted in parallel to a journal or any other conference / workshop that has proceedings.
  • Manuscripts must be original. Plagiarism of manuscripts should be checked before submission. It is suggested to keep the similarity index (Turnitin / iThenticate) of the manuscript within 20%.
  • Manuscripts must be typed in LaTeX. To download template please click here.
  • Length of the manuscript should not exceed 16 pages including the bibliography and well-marked appendices and supplementary materials.
  • Submission of manuscript does not guarantee acceptance. It will be decided by the Editors, Reviewers and the Organizing Committee.
  • Submissions not meeting these guidelines risk rejection without consideration of their merits.
