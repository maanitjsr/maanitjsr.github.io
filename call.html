Call for Papers

Conference Proceedings                    

Original and High Quality Research Papers offering novel research contributions on all aspects of Mathematical Analysis and Applications are solicited for submission to MAA 2025. Selected papers (after presentation and proper review process) may be considered for publication in one of the following Book Series:

  • Book Series: Springer Proceedings in Mathematics & Statistics (indexed in SCOPUS) [Proposal Submitted].

  • Editors: Prof. Ouayl Chadli (Florida Institute of Technology, USA), Dr. Sourav Das (National Institute of Technology Jamshedpur, India), Prof. Ram N. Mohapatra (University of Central Florida, USA), Prof. A. Swaminathan (Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee, India).
Information regarding Conference Proceedings will be Updated Soon. Please feel free to contact us if you need any further information.


  • Proceedings of MAA 2020 WAS PUBLISHED in the book series Springer Proceedings in Mathematics & Statistics and it is available online in the link.

Instructions for Authors (for Submission of Full Length Articles)

Please note the following before submitting the article.
  • ORIGINAL AND HIGH QUALITY RESEARCH PAPERS are invited to submit in MAA 2025.
  • Submissions must not substantially duplicate work that any of the authors has published elsewhere or has submitted in parallel to a journal or any other conference / workshop that has proceedings.
  • Manuscripts must be original. Plagiarism of manuscripts should be checked before submission. It is suggested to keep the similarity index (Turnitin / iThenticate) of the manuscript within 18%.
  • Manuscripts MUST BE TYPED in LaTeX Format. To download template please click here.
  • Length of the manuscript should not exceed 14 pages including the bibliography and well-marked appendices and supplementary materials.
  • Submission of manuscript does not guarantee acceptance. It will be decided by the Editors, Reviewers and the Organizing Committee.
  • Submissions not meeting these guidelines risk rejection without consideration of their merits.
  • There is NO PUBLICATION CHARGES.
