Call for Papers

Conference Proceedings

Proceedings of MAA 2020 has been published in the book series Springer Proceedings in Mathematics & Statistics and it is available online in the link.

Original papers offering novel research contributions on all aspects of Mathematical Analysis and Applications are solicited for submission to MAA 2022. Selected papers (after presentation and proper review process) may be considered for publication in one of the following international journals / Book Series:

  • Book Series: The proposal for the Conference Proceedings has been submitted to Springer for possible publication.
Please feel free to contact us if you need any further information.

Instructions for Authors

Please note the following before submitting the article.
  • Submissions must not substantially duplicate work that any of the authors has published elsewhere or has submitted in parallel to a journal or any other conference / workshop that has proceedings.
  • Manuscripts must be original. Plagiarism of manuscripts should be checked before submission. It is suggested to keep the similarity index (Turnitin / iThenticate) of the manuscript within 20%.
  • Manuscripts must be typed in LaTeX. To download template please click here.
  • Length of the manuscript should not exceed 12 pages including the bibliography and well-marked appendices and supplementary materials.
  • Submission of manuscript does not guarantee acceptance. It will be decided by the Editors, Reviewers and the Organizing Committee.
  • Submissions not meeting these guidelines risk rejection without consideration of their merits.
